InstaHelp is an emergency contact mobile application that detects natural human responses to stressful situations.

Upon activation, InstaHelp can send a text message to the user's designated emergency contacts and attract the attention of nearby bystanders by playing a loud siren from the user's phone and blinking the user's phone's flashlight.

InstaHelp provides a faster and more discreet emergency contact solution by removing the dependence on proprietary activation phrases that users may not recall in their time of need and attackers may recognize and realize the user's attempt to call for help.

With InstaHelp, safety is our speciality.
